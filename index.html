Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27 April 2026

SIGNAL ("we", "our", or "the game") is a mobile puzzle game developed by Cyberien. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

Information We Collect

SIGNAL is designed to be played without an account and without collecting personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or advertising IDs.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

SIGNAL saves your game progress (completed levels, energy, flux, daily quest state) locally on your device using standard browser and app storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

In-App Purchases

SIGNAL offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through the Google Play Store. We do not receive, store, or process your payment information. All billing is handled by Google in accordance with their own Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

SIGNAL does not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or social login providers. The game runs as a self-contained application.

Children's Privacy

SIGNAL is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of SIGNAL after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
