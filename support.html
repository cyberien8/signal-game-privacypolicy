Progress & Saves

How is my progress saved?

SIGNAL saves automatically — there's no account or login. Your progress is stored on your device and synced to our cloud against a random, anonymous device identifier.

I switched devices, or my progress is gone.

Because there's no account, progress is tied to your device's anonymous ID. Deleting the app or moving to a new device can start a fresh save. If you think your save was lost in error, email us and we'll do our best to help.

Purchases

How do in-app purchases work?

In-app purchases are optional — the full game is free. Core packs and deals are one-time consumables that add to your in-game balance. Payment is handled entirely by the App Store; we never see your card details.

I was charged but didn't receive my Cores.

First, open the shop and tap Restore Purchases. If your Cores still haven't arrived, email us with your App Store receipt or transaction ID and we'll sort it out.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, and select the purchase to request a refund.

Troubleshooting

The game won't load or crashes.

  • Fully close the app and reopen it.
  • Make sure you're on the latest version of iOS and of SIGNAL.
  • Restart your device.
  • As a last resort, reinstall the app (note: this may reset local progress).

If the problem continues, email us and describe what happens — screenshots help.
